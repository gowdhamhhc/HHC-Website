HashHackCode is an innovative tech education hub nestled in the vibrant city of Chennai, India. We are an energetic team of tech enthusiasts, educators, mentors and coding aficionados driven by a shared passion for transforming the world of tech education. In a world propelled by technological innovation, we believe the doors to progress should remain open to every individual. This is the very essence of HashHackCode – a beacon of opportunity that seeks to empower diverse cognitive minds with the invaluable skill of coding.

Through our unique learning methodologies and carefully curated curriculum, we are committed to offering inclusive tech education and mentorship to all individuals, regardless of their background, age and gender.

[  Our Story  ]

Group of College Students Studying at the Library In 2019, HashHackCode came to life with a visionary mission. Its founder, Manu Sekar, driven by a simple radical idea, saw the need to make tech education accessible for everyone. We recognized that many individuals with diverse cognitive abilities encountered challenges within the traditional higher education model, often leading to limited career avenues. Identifying this critical void, HashHackCode was born to bridge this gap, empowering learners of all backgrounds to access and excel in tech education.

Whether you're an autistic individual navigating the digital realm, a senior citizen embarking on a new technological journey, or someone with disabilities striving for independence, the ability to code bridges the gap between you and the digital world. Our mission is to make this bridge accessible to everyone, transcending limitations and creating a future where technology is an enabler, not a barrier.
